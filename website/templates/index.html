Unveiling My Path in Python Programming

My coding journey began when I was 14 years old, attending Zimuto High School in Masvingo, Zimbabwe. I studied Computer Studies for my O Level, and I loved it. We used Windows 95 and DOS machines back then, coding in BASIC and dBase III for databases. Internet access was very limited, and we mainly relied on our teachers and a few textbooks to learn programming. However, I still enjoyed it and pursued Computing at A Level, followed by a BSc in Computer Science at Midlands State University.

After graduation, I planned to work in telecoms, focusing on network engineering and systems administration to gain additional ICT experience before returning to software engineering and consultancy after five years. Although I entered consultancy after that period, it took me some time to return to coding. In 2015, I learnt Python through the Muzinda Hub Digital Skills programme, using the Treehouse online platform. Since then, I have coded in Python, developed websites with Django, and actively participated in the Python community.
